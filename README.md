## 🪪 About Me

Hi, I'm **Anand Bharadwaj** — an aspiring Data Analyst with a strong foundation in SQL, Python, Power BI, and Advanced Excel. I transitioned from a Mechanical Engineering background into data analytics, driven by my passion for working with data and solving real-world business problems.

I have hands-on experience in data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), ETL processes, and building interactive dashboards. I've worked on projects in domains like healthcare, logistics, and sports analytics, where I focused on extracting insights and supporting data-driven decision-making.

I am a quick learner with a strong analytical mindset, continuously improving my skills and exploring new tools in the data ecosystem. I'm currently seeking opportunities to apply my knowledge, contribute to impactful projects, and grow as a Data Analyst.
